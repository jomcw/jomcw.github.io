	Desserted:Desserted Is a 2D top-down turn based puzzle game where you the lonely town baker must venture through a forest full of tricky puzzle avoiding all threaten obstacles with nothing but a bag of all-purpose flour with you. You have think about your every move carefully as you progress, as each step you take, the enemies around you move as well.	Instructions:Left click to move your character.	Known issues:If the stun is used on an enemy not adjacent to the player, it may cause the enemy to be stunned even after the stun is cancelled. They could also present a circle of stars around them.

The intention is for the stun to only be used on adjacent enemies.	Music/Sounds:All music and sound FX were source through online royalty free sources.? www.Freesound.org
